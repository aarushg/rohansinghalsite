About

ROHAN
SINGHAL

ROHAN SINGHAL is passionate about telling stories that resonate on a deep emotional level and explore the human condition. He believes in the power of film to connect people, evoke empathy, and challenge perceptions.

Growing up in India with a love of watching films, he graduated from Vancouver Film School in 2024 — specializing in Producing and Post-Production, and working across short films, music videos, and branded content as Colorist, Editor, and Producer.

With a keen eye for detail and a dedication to authentic visual storytelling, ROHAN strives to create work that leaves a lasting impact on audiences.
